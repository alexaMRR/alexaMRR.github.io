What is Data Sovereignty?

Data Sovereignty is the idea that data, which is in essence information, has a value--and thus it has a rightful owner. Data should be maintained and controlled by those from who and where it’s collected. The National Library of Medicine describes data sovereignty as “a group or individual’s right to control and maintain their own data, which includes the collection, storage, and interpretation of data” (nnlm.gov)
